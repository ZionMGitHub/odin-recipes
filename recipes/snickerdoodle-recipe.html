Snickerdoodle Recipe

Description

These sweet treats are a great way to satisfy your sweet tooth. Around 4 dozen cookies are made from this recipe so you can share them with all your friends. The combination of the cinnamon-sugar taste and chewy interior will have everyone begging for another.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup of softened butter
  • 1/2 cup of shortening
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract
  • 2 3/4 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons of cream of tartar
  • 1 teaspoon of baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 2 tablespoons of white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons of ground cinnamon

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the butter, shortening, 1 1/2 cups of sugar, eggs, and vanilla extract with an electric mixer until smooth.
  3. Add in the flour, cream of tartar, baking soda, and salt into the bowl and mix until combined.
  4. Shape the dough by rounded spoonfuls into balls.
  5. Mix the sugar and cinnamon within a small bowl and roll dough balls within the mixture.
  6. Place the dough balls 2 inches apart on a greased baking pan.
  7. Bake for 8 to 10 minutes or until edges are slightly brown. Remove from baking pan to cool on wire racks.
