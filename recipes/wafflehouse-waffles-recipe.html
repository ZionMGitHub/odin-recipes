Waffle House Waffle Recipe

Description

Experience the taste of a tasty and crispy Waffle House waffle within your own house. This recipe works with any waffle mix as well and will allow you to make a around 2 to 3 golden brown waffles for you to enjoy for breakfast in the morning.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of Waffle House mix(any waffle mix will suffice)
  • 1 large egg
  • 3/4 cup of half and half

Steps

  1. Preheat waffle iron to desired temperature.
  2. In a large bowl, stir the egg and half and half with a whisk.
  3. Pour the waffle mix into the bowl and mix until blended.
  4. Ladle the batter into the preheated waffle iron. Cook the waffles until golden and crisp.
