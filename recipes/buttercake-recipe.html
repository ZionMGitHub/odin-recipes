Butter Cake Recipe

Description

By following this recipe, you'll be able to make a delicious butter cake which has a crumbly cake batter crust and a gooey cream cheese filling on top. This recipe is great for parties or gatherings as it produces around 20 servings. It's also easy to make as it only takes about 60 minutes between prep and cook time.

Ingredients

  • 1 package of boxed yellow cake mix
  • 1/2 cup of melted butter
  • 4 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract
  • 8 ounces of cream cheese
  • 4 cups of powdered sugar

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Grease 9x13-inch baking pan with butter.
  2. Make the cake layer: In a large bowl, combine the cake mix, melted butter, 1 teaspoon of vanilla, and 2 eggs with an electric mixer. flatten the mixture into the bottom of the baking pan in an even layer.
  3. Make the filling layer: Clean the large bowl and add in the cream cheese, remaining 2 eggs, and remaining 1 teaspoon vanilla and mix with the electric mixer while slowly beating in powdered sugar.Mix until the mixture it is smooth and no lumps remain.
  4. Pour the filling layer over the cake layer in the baking pan.(DO NOT MIX THE TWO LAYERS)
  5. Bake for about 40 to 45 minutes, until golden brown color. Check 5-10 minutes before it's supposed to be done. Allow to cool before serving.
